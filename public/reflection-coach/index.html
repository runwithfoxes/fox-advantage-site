The Reflection Coach
/01 pick a moment

Optional. Picking one scopes your thinking, but you can start writing without one.

/02 / first pass

Write what you think about it.

Reflection is about your thinking, not the events. Don't write a report. Write what you actually think about the moment you picked. The coach will push that thinking further on one angle of your choice.

Reflecting on
/03 / pick an angle

Pick an angle to explore.

Coaching goes one dimension deep, not five dimensions wide. Pick the angle where your thinking feels thinnest, and the coach will stay on it until something cracks.

/04 / go deep

Going deep on the dimension.

The coach stays on this one until something cracks. Reply honestly. Click the arrow to send. Click "I'm done" when you feel it land.

/05 / session end

That is the one for this session.

You can explore another angle on the same moment, or take the coach home.
